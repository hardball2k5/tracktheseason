MLB Pitching Board 2026

The Track The Season Pitching Board ranks all 30 MLB teams by rotation performance using Starter Signal data. Key metrics include Best 1-2 Punch (combined win pace for the top two starters), Rotation Cliff (gap between top-two pace and everyone-else pace), and Rotation Depth (how well the team performs beyond its top two starters).

What is a rotation cliff in baseball?

A rotation cliff is when a team's win pace drops dramatically when their top starters are not pitching. Track The Season quantifies this gap using actual team record in each pitcher's starts, not ERA or WHIP.
