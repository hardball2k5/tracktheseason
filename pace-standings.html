What are Pace Standings?
Traditional standings reward what happened over the entire season. Pace Standings compare a team's full-season pace with how they've played recently — identifying clubs that are accelerating, cooling off, or holding steady before the standings fully reflect those changes. Momentum Delta = L20 Pace − Season Pace.
