MLB Chaos Report 2026

The Track The Season Chaos Report shows how many MLB teams are tracking meaningfully differently from last season at the same point. The Chaos Index counts teams whose current win total is 5 or more wins ahead or behind their win total through the same number of games last year.

A Chaos Index above 18 means most of the league is off last year's script. Below 10 means the standings are following a predictable pattern. Updated after every game at tracktheseason.com/chaos-report.
