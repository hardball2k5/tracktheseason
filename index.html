How momentum labels work

Every label compares a team's last-20 game win% to their full-season win%. The gap shows which direction they're moving — regardless of standings.

🔥 Elite paceLast 20 win% ≥ .650. Historically dominant stretch.
📈 Heating upLast 20 ≥ .600 and meaningfully better than season average.
✅ Strong stretchLast 20 ≥ .575. Above a 93-win pace recently.
⬆️ Trending upLast 20 noticeably better than season average.
➡️ StableRecent form roughly matches overall season pace.
⬇️ Trending downLast 20 noticeably worse than season average.
📉 Cooling offLast 20 ≤ .420 and significantly below season average.
❄️ StrugglingLast 20 win% ≤ .350. Historically bad recent stretch.

Example: A team at 40–31 overall but 7–13 in their last 20 is 📉 Cooling off — the standings look fine but they're fading. Needs 10+ completed games.
