How momentum labels work
Each team gets two labels: where the season is headed (pace) and which direction right now (last 10 + 20 games).
🔥 SurgingLast 10 ≥ 65% and Last 20 ≥ 60%.
📈 Heating upLast 20 ≥ 60% and Last 10 ≥ 50%.
⬆️ RecoveringLast 20 ≤ 40% but Last 10 ≥ 60% — turning it around.
➡️ SteadyNo strong trend in either direction.
⬇️ FadingLast 20 ≥ 60% but Last 10 < 50% — momentum slipping.
📉 SlidingLast 20 ≤ 40% and Last 10 ≤ 40%.

Needs 10+ completed games. Pace = projected wins. Trend = last 10 + last 20 games.
